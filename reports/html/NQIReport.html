NQI(NovaCop Quality Index)


NQI indicates the health of your code quality with respect to best practices provided by NovaCop.

NQI for a project can be computed as follows:

1. First calculate the Incident Measure from the following formula

Incident Measure =NCWLOC/(P1 x 1.0 + P2 x 0.8 + P3 x 0.2 + P4*0.1)
where NCWLOC is Non Commented and non White space Lines of Code.
P1, P2, P3, P4 are Priority 1, Priority 2, Priority 3 and Priority 4 violations reported by Nova Cop.

2. NQI is obtained by mapping the Incident Measure to NQI Score in the following table

Incident Measure NQI Score
From To  
0 2 1
2 4 2
4 6 3
6 8 4
8 9 5
9 10 6
10 12 7
12 16 8
16 20 9
20   10

3. If NQI is below 2 , it is considered red. If it is below 5 , it is considered amber. if it is above 5, it is considered green.
